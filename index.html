Director of Product and Design at Supercritical

Previously led design at Heygo, Facebook, Bricklane and others

Supercritical is one of the world's leading carbon removal marketplaces. I led the creation of the marketplace from 0–1, from discovery to iteration; and it is now used by some of the world's largest enterprises. With Annabel Lake, Guy Moorhouse and Tom Rainford.

As design director, I oversaw Supercritical's brand evolution, working with talented designers like Aimee Randall, Guy Moorhouse, and Simon Whybray.

As director of product, one of my proudest achievments was creating one of the world's first end-to-end online purchase flows for durable carbon removal credits — an important step toward the goal of scaling to CDR 8 billion tonnes removed globally by 2050.

Having conducted hundreds of hours of customer discovery calls with climate leaders, and in order to not be a gatekeeper of this knowledge, I created the Customer Dæmon to let anyone in the company ask questions about our customers and get instant answers.

I co-founded the fintech Bricklane, and designed several iterations of its consumer apps and internal tooling, which today powers hundreds of millions of pounds of property transactions per annum. I also created the visual brand, partnering with Zoopla, Revolut, Monzo and others for distribution.

With Simon Whybray, James Shedden, and Martina Dawidowska.

Heygo was a virtual travel startup which raised $20m. As head of design I led design across the entire app — the user base of which skewed 50+, creating enjoyable and interesting UI challenges to solve. The postcard feature led to over 17 million postcards being created and shared.

At Meta (Facebook), I lead design at Spark AR. I designed a scalable system that surfaced actions that could be taken by Creators on a given Effect, drawn from their usage data.

When presenting my work to design leadership they said it “set the gold standard” for how new features should be designed at Facebook.

Peeps was an iOS app built with Alex Edge. It was featured in the App Store and gained over 10,000 users organically, in part down to an innovative sharing feature which let users send videos to friends who didn't yet have the app. I designed the app and built the web services in Express JS.
